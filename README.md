# AI Governance in Public Systems

A research-oriented project examining how Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming public systems, governance, and society. This repository explores the challenges, risks, governance frameworks, and future implications of AI in public administration and policymaking.

## Project Overview

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly being adopted in sectors such as healthcare, education, transportation, agriculture, law enforcement, and public service delivery. While AI offers significant opportunities for efficiency and innovation, it also raises concerns related to privacy, bias, transparency, accountability, cybersecurity, and democratic governance.

This project analyzes these opportunities and challenges through the lens of AI governance and examines major global governance frameworks.
